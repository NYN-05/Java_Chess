# Chess Game - Quick Controls Guide

## üñ±Ô∏è Moving Pieces - Two Methods

### Method 1: Click-to-Move (Classic)
1. **Click** on your piece to select it
   - Selected piece gets yellow highlight
   - Legal moves shown as green dots (moves) or red rings (captures)
2. **Click** on destination square
   - Piece slides smoothly to new position
   - Move appears in notation list

### Method 2: Drag-and-Drop (Modern) ‚ú® NEW!
1. **Click and Hold** on your piece
   - Piece "lifts" off the board (semi-transparent)
   - Legal moves shown as green dots or red rings
2. **Drag** to destination square
   - Piece follows your cursor
   - Shadow effect shows piece is "floating"
3. **Release** mouse button to drop
   - Piece slides smoothly to position
   - Move recorded automatically

**Tip**: Use whichever method feels more comfortable!

- **‚Ä†** symbol = Check

## üèÜ Game End Conditions

### Victory
- **Checkmate** - Opponent's king trapped
- **Timeout** - Opponent runs out of time

### Draw
- **Stalemate** - No legal moves, not in check
- **Threefold Repetition** - Same position 3 times
- **Fifty-Move Rule** - 50 moves without pawn move/capture
- **Insufficient Material** - Checkmate impossible

## üí° Tips for Beginners

### Using Drag-and-Drop
- **Press and hold** - Don't click twice!
- **Drag smoothly** - Piece follows your cursor
- **See targets** - Green dots show where you can go
- **Drop anywhere** - Invalid drops are ignored

### Using Click-to-Move
- **Click once** on your piece - Shows legal moves
- **Click again** on green dot - Makes the move
- **Click different piece** - Changes selection

### General Tips
- **Watch for check** - Orange pulse on king = danger!
- **Use undo freely** - Learn from mistakes (Ctrl+Z)
- **Take your time** - No rush (unless timer active!)
- **Follow the dots** - Green = safe move, Red = capture

## ‚ö° Power User Shortcuts

### Quick Actions
- **Double-click** piece - Shows legal moves
- **Click empty square** - Clears selection
- **Drag off board** - Cancels drag operation
- **Esc key** - Cancel current selection

### Efficient Play
1. Use **drag-and-drop** for speed
2. Use **click-to-move** for precision
3. Use **keyboard** for accessibility
4. Mix methods as needed!

## üéØ Common Scenarios

### "I want to capture that piece"
1. Click or drag your piece
2. Look for **red ring** around target
3. Click or drop on that square
4. Watch capture animation!

### "I'm in check!"
1. King square pulses **orange**
2. Green dots show escape squares
3. Move king or block the attack
4. Orange pulse disappears when safe

### "I made a mistake!"
1. Press **Ctrl+Z** immediately
2. Or use Edit ‚Üí Undo
3. Try a different move
4. Repeat up to 100 times!

### "I want to restart"
1. File ‚Üí Restart
2. Confirms if you're sure
3. Board resets to starting position
4. Move counter resets to 0

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### "Piece won't move"
- ‚úì Is it your turn? (Check status label)
- ‚úì Is it your piece? (White/Black)
- ‚úì Is the move legal? (Green dot present?)

### "Can't select piece"
- ‚úì Wrong color? (Must be your turn)
- ‚úì Animated? (Wait for animation to finish)
- ‚úì In check? (Limited moves available)

### "Drag not working"
- ‚úì Click and **hold** (don't release!)
- ‚úì Move mouse while holding
- ‚úì Release on destination

### "Timer running too fast"
- Check time control setting
- Some modes have short times (5 min)
- Use Custom for longer games
